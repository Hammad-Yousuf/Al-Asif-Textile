About Us

Al-Asif Textile Industries (ATI), a proud family owned business was established in 1993. We are one of the leading towel manufacturers in Pakistan. Our industrial plant houses state of the art equipment and high end machinery to ensure top notch quality and customer satisfaction. We have acquired a loyal clientele worldwide for exports. Majorly: GREECE, ENGLAND, ITALY, FRANCE, GERMANY, MAURATIUS, MALAYSIA and AMERICA (in no particular order). Our vision is to provide value added quality products that are sustainable in the long run. Al-Asif Textile Industries (ATI) launched its journey in 1993 with a vision of becoming the most recognized towel and home textile manufacturer of the country by offering the best blend of quality and efficiency. ATI has expanded its capacity, developed a skilled management team with efficient workforce, and integrated sophisticated technologies in order to become globally competitive. We are committed to provide quality product in competitive price. ATI has gathered immense reputation at home and abroad. Innovative business approach, foresight, sustainability, quality assurance and superior products have been the factors of our success. We are specialized in the production of all sorts of terry towels, terry bath robes, terry made-ups, kitchen towels and all other terry and non-terry as well home textile products, according to the demands of our valued customers. We have the expertise and facilities to offer advanced range of 100% cotton and blended products with various sizes, GSM, designs and colors. We are working relentlessly to expand our business through high ethical standards and being socially responsive. Keeping this in mind, we are setting off a new venture dedicatedly on modern and exclusive home textile for great fashion for each home. We are always in the quest of professionalism and perfection characterized by a continual pursuit for innovations and a focus towards nurturing lasting customer relationship.
